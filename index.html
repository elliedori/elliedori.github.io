Ayurveda

The Science of Living

Ayurveda is the ancient Indian system of healing and well-being. Ayurveda states that balance is the key to a healthy body, mind, and spirit. That balance can be found first by evaluating a person's dosha, or disposition, and then introducing balance through foods, exercises, and practices that temper what's excessive and encourage what's subdued. Although it's best to be examined by an Ayurvedic doctor to determine your dispostion, you can make an educated assumption by evaluating yourself. Most people are a mix of two dosha, with one being more dominant than the other.

Dosha Governing Element Physical Attributes Mental Attributes Emotional Attributes
Vata Air Natually thin body, dry skin, poor digestion, tends to be cold Fast-moving thoughts, creative Tends towards anxiety
Pitta Fire Naturally muscular, reddish skin, aggressive appetite, tends to be hot Sharp intellect, competitive Tends towards anger
Kapha Water/Earth Naturally plump, oily skin, hearty digestion, tends to sweat easily Slow and methodical thinking Tends towards depression
